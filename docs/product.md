# Syncrolly Product Brief

## One-line summary
Syncrolly helps creators and coaches manage serious supporters through gated messaging, paid access, booking, and AI-assisted inbox filtering.

## Primary users
- Fitness coaches
- Sales coaches
- Consultants
- Creators who monetize access to their time and replies

## Initial value proposition
- Reduce spam and low-value message requests
- Prioritize paying supporters and qualified leads
- Give creators one place for messaging, booking, and supporter access

## Initial MVP
- Creator and supporter profiles
- Gated messaging
- Inbox filters
- Notifications
- Booking request stub
- Mocked data only

## Not in v1
- Full payments integration
- Full AI classification
- Community feed
- Course hosting
